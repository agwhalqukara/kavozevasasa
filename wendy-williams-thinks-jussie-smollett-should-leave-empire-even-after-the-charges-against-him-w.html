    Wendy Williams Thinks Jussie Smollett Should Leave Empire Even After The Charges Against Him W

    The charges against Jussie Smollett may have been dropped but Wendy Williams thinks he should leave Empire in the aftermath of his scandal anyway! The talk show host believes he will be judged for the rest of his life and that will, of course, affect his career a lot too.

    21 Jul 2024, 00:00

    The charges against Jussie Smollett may have been dropped but Wendy Williams thinks he should leave Empire in the aftermath of his scandal anyway! The talk show host believes he will be ‘judged for the rest of his life’ and that will, of course, affect his career a lot too.

    She also said that the whole drama surrounding him has created a divide on the set of Empire.

    As you remember, the actor was accused of staging a hate crime against him because he was unhappy with his salary on the show.

    This has not been proven to be true, and Jussie has vehemently denied it.

    Wendy told her studio audience: ‘He’s the luckiest man in the world for not going to jail. Jussie will be judged for the rest of his life. I venture to say, even if he gets acting roles, they are probably not going to be productions. They are just going to throw him in stuff that he will be forced to take because he will need money.’

    The host still managed to find a silver lining in all of this, however.

    ‘Jussie’s thing with the law has definitely benefitted in terms of everyone talking about you, you are the lead story on everything. If I were him I would quit [Empire]. Let him just walk away and not make the staff and crew and his fellow actors suffer because everyone is divided on the set. It makes for a nasty situation when a nasty situation is going on.’

    After Smollett’s lawyer confirmed via HollywoodLife that all the charges against his client had been dropped, the actor also insisted that, throughout the investigation, he has been ‘truthful on every level.’
