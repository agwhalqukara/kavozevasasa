    Closer Weekly can exclusively reveal that Shayne Lamas and Nik Richie welcomed their second child, a baby boy, via surrogate, just after midnight on July 4.

    As Closer exclusively revealed in February, Lorenzo Lamas’ wife, Shawna Craig, 29, carried the baby for her stepdaughter Shayne, also 29, who’s unable to have children following a near-fatal ectopic pregnancy in 2014.

    Nik, Shayne, Lorenzo and Shawna in March 2011.

    Weighing in at 6 pounds, 4 ounces, “the new bundle of joy arrived via natural water birth and everyone is healthy and happy,” a source close to Shayne and her husband, Nik, 36, confides to Closer.

    RELATED: Lorenzo Lamas Balances Work and Family Life in His Beverly Hills Home

    And the pregnancy certainly brought the two moms closer together. “Shawna’s not my stepmother, she’s like my soul sister,” Shayne, 29, raved to Closer in February. “I look at her like an angel that blessed my whole family, including my father [who’s 57]. I have just been given the biggest blessing in the world and I’ll always be grateful.”

    This is Shayne and Nik’s second child. They also have a three-year-old-daughter named Press.
