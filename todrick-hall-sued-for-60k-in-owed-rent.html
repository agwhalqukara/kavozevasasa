    Celebrity Big Brother star Todrick Hall is being sued for allegedly owing $60,000 in rent for a home he claimed he had purchased.

    Avi Lavian and Orna Lavian Hall agreed to pay $30,000 a month in rent in a court document obtained by People. He did not pay for February and March of this year, so the couple gave him a three-day notice on March 3 to pay rent or leave the property.

    Last February, he posted a video to Youtube claiming that he had bought the 5 bedroom, 8 bathroom property.

    “When I saw this one I fell in love. I’m talking that Allie and Noah Notebook kind of love.”

    The home was robbed last July.

    “I am ok. This happened right after my cat sitter left for the night,” he wrote. “I’m extremely saddened that most likely this was done by someone I know and trust, but my cats are safe…and that’s what matters most to me.”
