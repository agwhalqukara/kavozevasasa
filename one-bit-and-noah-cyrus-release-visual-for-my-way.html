    One Bit and Noah Cyrus release visual for 'My Way'

    Back in November, British production duo One Bit teamed up with US star Noah Cyrus for a thumping new dance track called My Way. Whilst weve been loving the song since then, My Way has now been given an extra boost as the official music video was released today.

    05 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Back in November, British production duo One Bit teamed up with US star Noah Cyrus for a thumping new dance track called ‘My Way’. Whilst we’ve been loving the song since then, ‘My Way’ has now been given an extra boost as the official music video was released today.

    The pastel pink and blue visual sees Noah host an eccentric dinner party. Dressed in a frilly pink outfit, Noah and her guests show off some alternative dance moves and they look like they’re having an absolute ball whilst doing so. The visual has a fun vibe and suits the electric nature of the song perfectly.

    Speaking about the collaboration, One Bit said: “We originally co-wrote ‘My Way’ to a completely different track with Katya Edwards — a great songwriter friend of ours. The vocal from that original version was a lot slower, so we sped it up and put it onto the early stages of what became ‘My Way.’ When it was all together in its current form we got in touch with Noah to see if she was up for featuring on it. Thankfully, she said yes!”

    Check out the official video for ‘My Way’ below.

    Since its release, the track has been streamed over three million times on Spotify alone. ‘My Way’ was Nick Grimshaw’s ‘Tune of the Week’ during its release week and has since made it to Radio 1’s B List playlist. If you think the song sounds similar, it might be down to the fact that it samples Mylo’s classic dance anthem ‘Drop the Pressure’.

    Last week, One Bit and Noah also dropped an acoustic version of ‘My Way’, which you can listen to below.
