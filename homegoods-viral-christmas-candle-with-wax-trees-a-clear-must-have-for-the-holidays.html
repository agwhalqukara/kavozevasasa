    Care and Safety Tips for Candle Use

    Candles are a great way to set the mood and create a cozy atmosphere during the holiday season. However, it is important to use them safely and properly to prevent accidents. Here are some tips for taking care of your candles:

    • Trim the wick: Before lighting the candle, trim the wick to 1/4 inch. This will help prevent soot and ensure an even burn.
    • Use a proper holder: Always use a holder that is designed for the size and shape of the candle. This will prevent the candle from tipping over and causing a fire.
    • Keep away from flammable materials: Make sure the candle is placed on a stable surface away from curtains, furniture, and other flammable materials.
    • Never leave a burning candle unattended: Always keep an eye on the candle while it is burning. Never leave it unattended, especially around children or pets.
    • Extinguish the candle properly: Use a candle snuffer or a metal lid to extinguish the candle. Blowing out the candle can cause hot wax to splatter and start a fire.

    By following these simple tips, you can enjoy the warm glow of candles while keeping your home safe during the holiday season.
