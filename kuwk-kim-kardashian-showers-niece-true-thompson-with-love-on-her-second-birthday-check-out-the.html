    Today is True Thompson’s second birthday and among the many people in the family and all over social media who sent their well wishes was, of course, auntie Kim Kardashian! The KUWK star made sure to share a number of sweet pics of sister Khloe Kardashian and her ex, Tristan Thompson’s daughter, showing of their incredible bond.

    True is barely two but it turns out that she’s really close with her aunt Kim ever since birth since she’s made sure to be in her life as much as possible.

    Earlier today, Kim marked her special day amid this quarantine by sharing with her millions of followers no less than 10 adorable pics of the toddler.

    Of course, in the caption, Kim mentioned how disappointed she was about not being able to see True on her birthday due to the quarantine orders.

    Regardless, she made sure to shower her with love via social media, writing: ‘My sweet baby True. Happy 2nd Birthday! I wish we could all be there with you to celebrate you today! I love you so much precious girl! ✨Looking through pictures to post made me so happy, this cousin bond is so special and will last forever ✨,.’ Aww….What a sweet family relationship!

    Furthermore, there was also one photo that featured Kim herself cuddling her sister’s adorable daughter.

    Khloe was quick to respond to the sweet post in her daughter’s name, writing in the comment section: ‘Awwww we love you auntie Kiki!!!! She’s so loved it’s all we could ever ask for.’

    Just the day before, the proud mother posted some hilarious vids of True being her cheerful self while in her playhouse, making pretend cupcakes and pasta! So cute!
