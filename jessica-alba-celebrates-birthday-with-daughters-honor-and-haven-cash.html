    So much love! Jessica Alba celebrated her 33rd birthday on Monday, April 28, with her beloved husband Cash Warren and their adorable daughters Honor, 5, and Haven, 2.

    Producer Warren, 35, posted a sweet creative mirror photo of birthday girl Alba holding their girls on her lap. “Happy Birthday to my one and only,” Warren wrote in his Instagram post. “Thank you for being a constant reminder for what’s important in life…family & friendship. I love you more than my words can ever express.”

    Alba also shared an Instagram picture of her husband Monday and wrote, “I’ve officially spent a decade of my life w this hottie.” The Honest Company co-founder gushed of Warren, “Love of my life,” and “u make me so happy.”

    Alba also shared a picture of a homemade birthday card from Haven and Honor. “#handmade #homemade #birthday card from my lil angelpies,” she wrote beside the pink paper embellished with cut-out hearts and candles.

    The Sin City franchise star opened up to Redbook magazine in its April issue about life at home with her kids—which is always affectionate, she said. Alba was shocked to learn some parents don’t kiss their kids on the lips. “Really? Why?!” Alba asked. “It’s your baby. People allow dogs to lick at their mouths.”

    Alba also shared in the interview that some of her best pals are parents in her star-studded Mommy and Me group. The actress—who’s close to fellow celebrity moms like Gwen Stefani, Nicole Richie, and Jaime King—added, “They’re some of my closest girlfriends.”

    In the same interview, Alba told Redbook that her hubby makes fun of her social media obsession. “All he watches is sports,” she jokingly lamented about Warren, to whom she’s been married since 2008. “Though he says I’m addicted to Instagram, so it goes both ways!”
